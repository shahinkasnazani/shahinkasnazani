About Me

My name is Shahin Kasnazani Khadim. I am a follower of Sufism, the mystical dimension of Islam that focuses on the inward search for God and seeks to embrace the teachings of unity, peace, and love. My journey is centered around the belief that all religions, from Prophet Adam to Prophet Mohammed, are branches of the same tree, sharing a common divine message.

Sufism & The Unity of Religions

Sufism, at its heart, is about the realization of Divine Oneness. It teaches us that the light of God shines through all the prophets, from Adam to Mohammed, illuminating the path for humanity. This path transcends religious boundaries and unites believers of all faiths under the one roof of God's love.

In the teachings of Sufism, we find the essence of peace, tolerance, and unity. It encourages us to look beyond the surface and embrace the spiritual connection that binds all religions together. We honor the prophets not only as messengers but as bearers of a shared divine truth, leading humanity toward love, compassion, and understanding.

The current master of Sufism is Hazrat Al-Shaikh Shamsudeen Muhammad Nahro Al-Kasnazani Al-Qadiri Al-Hussaini, who continues to guide and inspire followers on the path of spiritual enlightenment.
